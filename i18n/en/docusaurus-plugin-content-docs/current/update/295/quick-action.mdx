A new addition to the <Cmd sid="common.triggerAndAction" /> menu, <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> allows you to quickly control the operation of the door in an emergency situation.

By adding the <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> button in the <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" /> menu, you gain the ability to conveniently control multiple doors simultaneously. Simply click the <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> button on the BioStar 2 main screen to initiate the command.

## Adding the Trigger & Action to Use as Quick Action

1. Click <Cmd sid="common.setting" /> → <Cmd sid="common.triggerAndAction" />.

1. Click <Cmd sid="button.func.addTriggerAndAction" />, and then set each item to add the desired action.

    <Image src='/img/biostar-295-trigger-action.png' caption />

1. Select <Cmd sid="triggerAction.schedule" /> to <Cmd>Always</Cmd>, check <Cmd sid="triggerAction.quickAction" />, and then click <Cmd sid="button.apply" /> to save settings.

:::info

* <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> is only available when the <Cmd sid="triggerAction.schedule" /> is <Cmd>Always</Cmd>.

* By selecting <Cmd sid="triggerAction.quickAction" />, you can create an action without the need to specify an <Cmd sid="setting.triggerAction.event" />.

:::

## Adding the Quick Action Button to the Biostar 2 Main Screen

1. Click <Cmd sid="common.setting" /> → <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" />.

1. In the <Cmd sid="common.quickActionLayout" />, click <Cmd sid="button.plusAdd" /> to enter and select each item.

    <Image src='/img/biostar-295-add-new-quick-action.png' className='none' caption />

    * <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.operatorLevel" />: Select the <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.operatorLevel" /> that can run the added <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" />. You can select multiple options.

    * <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.triggerAction" />: Select the <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.triggerAction" /> to run. You can select multiple options.

    * <Cmd sid="settings.customInterface.quickAction.confirmBeforeRun" />: Enabling this option prompts a confirmation pop-up when pressing the <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> button on the main screen, asking whether to proceed with running the <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" />.

1. Click <Cmd sid="button.apply" /> to save the settings.

1. Click <Cmd sid="button.apply" /> on the <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" /> screen. Check the confirmation pop-up and click <Cmd>Yes</Cmd>. The <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> button added to the top bar of BioStar 2 will be created.

    <Image src='/img/biostar-295-quick-action-top-bar.png' className='none' caption />

		:::info

		* Up to 4 <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> buttons can be added to the top bar of the main screen.

		* The <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> button that appears may vary depending on the <Cmd sid="user.bioStarOperator" /> set for the user.

		:::

1. Clicking the <Cmd sid="common.quickAction" /> button, created on the top bar of the main screen, will execute the predefined actions set in <Cmd sid="common.triggerAndAction" />.

## Editing the Quick Action Buttons

1. Click <Cmd sid="common.setting" /> → <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" />.

1. Click the <Image src='/img/common/ico-quick-action-button-edit.png' ico alone /> icon in the right column of the Quick Action button to edit, then edit the desired part and click <Cmd sid="button.apply" />.

    <Image src='/img/biostar-295-custom-interface.png' caption />

1. Click <Cmd sid="button.apply" /> on the <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" /> screen. Check the pop-up and click <Cmd>Yes</Cmd> to apply the modifications.

## Deleting the Quick Action Button

1. Click <Cmd sid="common.setting" /> → <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" />.

1. Click the <Image src='/img/common/ico-quick-action-button-del.png' ico alone /> icon in the right column of the Quick Action button to delete.

    <Image src='/img/biostar-295-quick action-del.png' className='none' caption />

1. Check the deletion confirmation pop-up and click <Cmd sid="button.yes" />.

1. Click <Cmd sid="button.apply" /> on the <Cmd sid="common.customInterface" /> screen. Check the pop-up and click <Cmd>Yes</Cmd> to apply the deletion.
